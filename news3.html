Leclerc’s Heartbreak At Home

Charles Leclerc’s Monaco curse continues, and this time, Ferrari’s strategy team handed him the heartbreak. Starting P2 behind Lando Norris, Leclerc looked poised to finally challenge for victory on home soil. But a mid-race Virtual Safety Car changed everything. While McLaren executed a flawless stop for Norris, Ferrari hesitated—again. By the time Leclerc was called in, the VSC had ended, and the time loss was brutal. He rejoined behind Russell and even lost time defending from Verstappen. From there, it was processional. A weekend that began with hope ended in another missed opportunity, not because of Leclerc’s driving—but because of Ferrari’s indecision on the pit wall. Monaco may be his home, but until Ferrari gets it right, it won’t be his kingdom.
